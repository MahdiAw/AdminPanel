Aquatricity Features

Assisting you throughout your consumption optimization journey

Get Tips & Awareness

The Aquatricity app offers facts on the national and global water and energy situation, as well as specific tips that increase the eco-friendliness of your daily routine and help you become an environmental changemaker.

Smart Meters

Locally designed and assembled, our WiFi-connected meters measure your consumption values and upload them to the mobile app so that you can easily track your daily, monthly, and yearly consumption.

Solution Suggestions

Based on the collected data, our algorithms recommend devices and behavior modifications to help you optimize your consumption, therefore reducing your bills and saving the environment.

Enjoyable Interface

Designed in a way that reflects our identity, the Aquatricity app offers a user-friendly experience with vibrant colors appealing to the different customer age ranges to give them a feeling of energy and freshness.
